The World's Easiest Omelette

Description

There are a million different ways to make an omelette. But what are you going to make when you're tired after work? An omelette that only takes two minutes of course.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Cheese
  • 2 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Oil a small circular frying pan and turn a hotplate on high, and the grill of your oven so they can be warming up.

  2. Break eggs into a bowl and whisk with a fork until there is no difference in colour between the yolk and the white.

  3. Pour into pan and cook on the hotplate until the bottom of the omelette is set.

  4. Place cheese on top and place under the oven grill.

  5. When cheese has melted, serve as is, or with some toppings. Feel free to mix and match some from the list below or make up your own pairings!

    6. Possible Toppings:

    Chilli sauce, Hoisin Sauce, Mayo, BBQ Sauce, Salad Cream.
